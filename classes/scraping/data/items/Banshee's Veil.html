Banshee's Veil

Stats

+80 ability power
+10 ability haste
+45 magic resistance

Passive

Unique – Annul: Grants a spell shield that blocks the next hostile ability (40 (restarts upon taking damage from champions) second cooldown).

    Similar items

    Edge of Night
    Morellonomicon
    Zhonya's Hourglass

    Notes

    • Annul is placed on cooldown when the user dies.
    • Annul will be active on clones of the user.

    Strategy

    Main article: Crowd control
    • Banshee's Veil is a strong item against magic damage but is most valued for its ability to prevent crowd control. As such it is highly recommended to build it when you are being focused by crowd control effects.

    V12.12
    • Bug Fix: Spell shield VFX is no longer occasionally not shown if the enemy owner was not seen for a while.
    V11.7
    • Bug Fix: Fixed a bug where the spellshield would not be affected by item haste, only its icon.
    V11.6
    • Bug Fix: Annul cooldown now properly displays in the HUD.

    • Total cost increased to 2600 from 2500.
      • Combine cost increased to 700 from 400.
    • Ability power increased to 80 from 65.
    V11.1
    • Bug Fix: Passive cooldown no longer ignores item haste granted from Ingenious Hunter.
    V10.23
    • Updated icon.
    • New:
      • Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Verdant Barrier + 400 = 2500.
      • Stats: 65 ability power, 45 magic resistance, 10 ability haste.
      • Unique Passive - Annul: Grants a spell shield that blocks the next ability. This shield refreshes after no damage is taken from enemy champions for 40 seconds.
      • Limited to 1 Banshee's Veil.
    • Old:
      • Recipe: Fiendish Codex + Null-Magic Mantle + Blasting Wand + 800 = 3000.
      • Stats: 75 ability power, 60 magic resistance, 10% cooldown reduction.
